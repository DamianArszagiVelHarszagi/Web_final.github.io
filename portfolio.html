Portfolio

I started my journey in 2020 when COVID hit. With a lot of free time on my hands, I began watching YouTubers who did video editing. I instantly loved it, and my dad noticed. He gave me access to his Adobe Creative Cloud account. At first, I made Fortnite montages for myself and my friends. They weren't great, but I improved every month.

About a year later, I met a friend who helped me get better. He also introduced me to Adobe After Effects. It was tough at first, but after a few months, I got the hang of it and started making animations and Fortnite montages. Then, I took a 1.5-year break due to burnout.

Later, a friend introduced me to a football player looking for a video editor for his clips. I got paid to do something I loved. Over the years, I've made around 100 videos.

When university started, I didn't have much time to edit anymore. But just before the second semester, I found this major that includes video editing and jumped at the chance to enroll.
