Hobbies

My main hobby is football; it always has been and always will be. I've been passionate about it for as long as I can remember. When I was six years old, I joined the football club KV Zuun and spent twelve years there. Unfortunately, I had to quit when I was fourteen because I couldn't balance school and football, a decision I regret to this day.

My favorite football club is FC Barcelona, primarily because of Lionel Messi. Even after he moved to another club, I continued to support Barcelona. My dream is to attend a football match at their stadium, Camp Nou.
